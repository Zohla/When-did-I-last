About

Hi! My name is Maren and I'm a maker of things!

I made this web app because I missed a low-pressure, low-maintenance task tracking app, and I wanted to challenge myself to build one.

I always have a hundred things to do. But tracking every single task or having an app that constantly tells me that my tasks are overdue is just stressful.

"Sometimes I just want to know the last time I did something...that doesn't mean I'm gonna do it again right away."

When did I last? makes it easier to prioritize because sometimes the sun is shining, and you really just have the time to change the sheets OR clean under the kids' beds.

I started this project in May 2022 and still have features I'd like to add and styles I want to update.
