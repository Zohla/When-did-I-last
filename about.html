About

Hi! My name is Maren and I'm a maker of things!

I made this web app because I was missing a low preassure, low maintainance task tracking app.

Like you, I always have a hundred things to do. But the thought of tracking every single thing, or having an app constantly telling me that all my tasks are over due just adds to the stress.

Sometimes I just want to know when the last time I did something was. That doesn't mean I'm gonna do it just then.

It makes it easier to prioritize, because sometimes the sun is shining and you really just have the time to change the sheets OR clean under the kids beds.
