Helps keep track of you tasks

Do you struggle to keep track of the last time you did something? Even if you know you should do it fairly routinly?

When did I last? helps you track when you do something, when you did it last time, and optionally set intervals and reminders for your tasks.

This is just a demo, and your data is not saved!
